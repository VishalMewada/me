Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects do you work on?

I specialize in instructional design and eLearning development, including interactive courses, training programs, mobile learning solutions, and learning management systems. I work with various industries including corporate training, education, and healthcare.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A simple eLearning module might take 2-4 weeks, while comprehensive training programs can take 2-3 months. I always provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes! I have experience working with global teams and clients from various countries. I'm comfortable with different time zones and can accommodate international project requirements and cultural considerations.

What tools and technologies do you use?

I work with Articulate Storyline & Rise 360, Vyond, WalkMe, and various AI-driven content development tools. I also have experience with learning management systems and can integrate with your existing platforms.
